“Mini Mountain Suite”

Connected by a permanently closed and insulated door to the Mountain Suite, the MMS enjoys mountain views and southern exposure which fills it with light.  It is a cozy room with two closets located on our second floor. It will be unfurnished, though we may have a few things we can donate. Rent for this room is $678 per month, plus $150 for food and $65 for utilities up front every month. (All-inclusive monthly cost = $893) New members are expected to pay $50 to buy into the co-op and assume ownership of resources. This is refunded upon move-out, when replaced by a new member. New members are also expected to put down a $375 security deposit, which is refundable but depreciates $5 for every month living here (i.e. if you live here for 6 months you will receive $345 upon move-out). Available August 1st, but move-in date is flexible and we are willing to prorate rent.

Total cost to move in = $1318

“Mountain Suite” –  Space for 1 or 2

This is the master bedroom of the house. It enjoys south and east facing windows with mountain views and tons of sun. The Mountain Suite comprises two “rooms,” one sleeping/living area big enough for a bed and couch and one kitchenette.  The kitchenette has it’s own sink but no oven or burners.  The kitchenette has wonderful light and has been used as both an office and den by previous tenants. Rent for this space is $1100 per month, plus $150 for food and $65 for utilities up front every month. (All-inclusive monthly cost = $1315) New members are expected to pay $50 to buy into the co-op and assume ownership of resources. This is refunded upon move-out, when replaced by a new member. New members are also expected to put down a $375 security deposit, which is refundable but depreciates $5 for every month living here (i.e. if you live here for 6 months you will receive $345 upon move-out). We add an additional $200 per person over 1 per space to account for communal space use. Available January 1st, but move-in date is flexible and we are willing to prorate rent.

Total cost to move in single: rent+food+utilities+deposit+buy in = $1740 Total cost to move in couple: rent+$200+2food+2utilities+2deposit+2buy in =  $2,580
